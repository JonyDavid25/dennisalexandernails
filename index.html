ABOUT DENNIS ALEXANDER

Hi, I’m Dennis Alexander — a mobile nail technician with over 9 years of experience in the beauty industry, including 4 years working in the prestigious Kensington area.

Throughout my career, I’ve had the pleasure of working with a wide range of clients, including celebrities, delivering high-end nail services tailored to individual needs.
I specialise in luxury manicures and pedicures, offering a professional, hygienic, and personalized experience — right at the comfort of your home.

Whether you’re looking for flawless nails or a moment of pampering, I’m here to bring the salon to you.
Book your appointment today. Your nails deserve the best.
